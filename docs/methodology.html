Methodology

Structura & Ratio

The goal of the Country Index is to construct a comprehensive, multi-dimensional representation of sovereign entities. This is achieved by decomposing national complexity into distinct analytical spaces—legal, economic, physical, and social—thereby rendering the dynamics of states intelligible and comparable.

The Object of Inquiry

Codifying the regulatory frameworks, legislative structures, and juridical constraints.

Guiding Principles

Inference & Data

"Use representation forms that can be powered by large-scale data inference—taking raw data such as laws, regulations, or records, and inferring structured metrics from them."

Analytical Techniques

Representation Forms

Actor Set Event Representation

Chronological mapping of discrete actions taken by identified agents within the system.

State Snapshot Representation

Cross-sectional analysis capturing the static condition of variables at a specific point in time.

Technique → Product Matrix

Mapping productive capabilities against outputs to identify industrial requirements and opportunities.

Infrastructure Graph

Topological representation of critical systems, nodes, and the logistical connections between them.
